## 📈 统计检验细节

### Wilcoxon秩和检验（Mann-Whitney U Test）

**检验目的**: 非参数检验，不假设数据分布，适合云调度实验数据

**原假设H0**: CBO和LSCBO-Fixed性能无差异
**备择假设H1**: LSCBO-Fixed性能优于CBO

**结果汇总**:

| 规模 | p-value | 显著性星号 | 拒绝H0? | 结论 |
|------|---------|-----------|---------|------|
| M=100 | 0.0283 | * | ✅ Yes | LSCBO-Fixed > CBO |
| M=500 | 0.0090 | ** | ✅ Yes | LSCBO-Fixed > CBO |
| M=1000 | 0.0090 | ** | ✅ Yes | LSCBO-Fixed > CBO |
| M=2000 | 0.0090 | ** | ✅ Yes | LSCBO-Fixed > CBO |

**显著性标注**:
- `*`: p < 0.05 (显著)
- `**`: p < 0.01 (高度显著)
- `***`: p < 0.001 (极度显著)

### Cohen's d效应量

**计算公式**:
```
Cohen's d = (M_CBO - M_LSCBO) / pooled_std
```

**合并标准差**:
```
pooled_std = sqrt(((n1-1)*var1 + (n2-1)*var2) / (n1+n2-2))
```

**效应量汇总**:

| 规模 | Cohen's d | 类别 | 标准化差异 | 实际差异 |
|------|-----------|------|-----------|---------|
| M=100 | 1.9929 | Large | 2.0σ | -184.98s |
| M=500 | 2.7677 | Large | 2.8σ | -654.25s |
| M=1000 | **5.5120** | **超大** | **5.5σ** | **-1024.76s** |
| M=2000 | 2.2142 | Large | 2.2σ | -531.18s |

## 📚 统计方法参考

### Wilcoxon秩和检验

**文献**: Wilcoxon, F. (1945). Individual comparisons by ranking methods. *Biometrics bulletin*, 1(6), 80-83.

**适用性**:
- 非参数检验，不假设正态分布
- 适合小样本（n=5）
- 对异常值鲁棒

### Cohen's d效应量

**文献**: Cohen, J. (1988). *Statistical power analysis for the behavioral sciences* (2nd ed.). Hillsdale, NJ: Lawrence Erlbaum Associates.

**效应量标准**:
- d = 0.2: small effect
- d = 0.5: medium effect
- d = 0.8: large effect
